# Enterprise AI Case Studies

## Purpose

One of the most effective ways to understand Responsible AI is by studying real-world failures.

This document captures publicly documented incidents involving Generative AI systems, analyzes the underlying causes, and identifies which evaluation techniques, guardrails, or governance processes could have prevented them.

The objective is not to criticize individual organizations, but to learn from production incidents and improve the design of enterprise AI systems.

---

# Case Study Template

Each case study will follow the same structure.

## Business Context

Describe the AI application and the business problem it was intended to solve.

## What Happened?

Summarize the publicly reported incident.

## Root Cause Analysis

Identify the likely technical and operational causes.

## Evaluation Gaps

Which evaluations could have detected the issue?

Examples:

- Groundedness
- Faithfulness
- Bias
- Toxicity
- Prompt Injection
- Business Rule Validation
- Human Review

## Potential Guardrails

What additional safeguards could have reduced the risk?

## Lessons Learned

Key takeaways for enterprise AI teams.
